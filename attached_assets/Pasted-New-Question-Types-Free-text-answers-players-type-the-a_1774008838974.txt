New Question Types

Free-text answers — players type the answer, AI grades it (great for spelling, dates, names)
Ordering questions — drag items into the correct sequence
Matching pairs — connect terms to definitions
Audio questions — play a sound clip or music, players guess
Poll mode — non-scored opinion questions with live word cloud
New Game Modes

Team Mode — split players into teams, compete collectively; great for classroom management
Assignment Mode — teacher assigns a quiz as homework with a deadline; players complete it solo on their own time
Relay Race — each team member answers one question in a chain; miss your answer and you cost your team
AI Features

Generate from document — paste text or upload a PDF, AI extracts questions automatically
AI hint system — players can tap "Hint" for a clue, but it costs points
Explanation mode — after each question, AI shows a short explanation of why the answer is correct
Difficulty slider — AI rewrites your quiz harder or easier on demand
